FACILITIES

CLASSROOMS

Our spacious and modern classrooms are designed to create a conducive learning environment. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and comfortable seating, our classrooms provide an interactive space for students to engage in discussions, presentations, and collaborative projects.

LIBRARY

The school's library is a hub of knowledge and exploration. With a diverse collection of books, magazines, and digital resources, students have the opportunity to expand their horizons and engage in research and reading. Our library provides a peaceful and welcoming environment for individual study or group collaboration.
