Instructions to fill Application form for LKG 2023-24 Submission of applications: 19th December 2022 to 05th January 2023 Applications have to be submitted on or before 05th January 2023

Steps to fill in the online application form. Please read the same carefully before proceeding.

Step 1 : Register yourself onto the admission portal by clicking on the Register button and filling in the required details.

Step 2: You will receive an email on your registered email id.

Step 3. Confirm your registration by clicking on the link in the email.

Step 4: You will be automatic-ally redirected to the admission portal with a message of confirmation. Click on the Login button with your registered email-id and password. Check spam or junk folder if email is not received in the inbox or updates tab for Gmail.

Step 5: Fill in the form and upload the relevant documents.

Step 6: If you want to review the form please click on Save as Draft on the last page.

Step 7: Click on the Submit button and make the payment through the payment gateway.

Note : Once the form is submitted you will not be able to make any changes to the application form

Step 8: You will get a receipt by email confirming your transaction.

Step 9: Login to the admission portal and download the PDF which will be generated.

Step 10: If the PDF is not available due to high server load you can login after 12 hours and download the PDF and print it for your reference.

Points to Note

* Birth Certificate issued by BBMP with child’s name or for other states an equivalent statutory authority.

* CSI Membership Certificate issued by the Presbyter-in-charge of the Church – KCD (if CSI member) indicating the period of membership.

* Baptism Certificate of child (if CSI member).

* Church Membership Certificate (if Christian other than CSI).

* Caste Certificate (in case of SC / ST) issued by Tahsildar.

* Passport photo of child max size 170x132 pixels

* Parents Address proof – Aadhaar/ DL/ Voters ID/ Passport - any one proof.

* Self attested certificate of parent’s highest educational qualification.

* Old boy applying for their ward’s under the Old boy category should upload 10th Standard marks card or Transfer Certificate(TC) - TC is preferred.

* Staff of BCBS/BCGS/IT/GOVT Category - Present Working Certificate to be attached. In case it is difficult to get the working certificate during the application period, kindly upload a self-attested latest salary certificate or statement. Working certificate will have to be presented during verification of documents.

* Under Sibling Category – If Sibling studying in BCBS need to mention name and class presently studying and also upload latest term marks card.

Terms and Conditions

Incomplete forms will be rejected.

Forms with incorrect information will not be considered for the draw of lots.

Application for admission will not guarantee a seat.

The school will communicate admission details via email.

Attested copies of the corporation Birth Certificate must bear the child’s name.

Parents of CSI candidates must submit the Baptism certificate of the child and the parent’s church membership letter indicating the period of membership (issued by the Presbyter-in-charge).

Self-attested photocopies of the parents; (both) highest educational qualifications are mandatory.

Caste Certificate (in case of SC / ST) issued by Tahsildar.

BCBS/BCGS /KCD Staff / Govt / IT to attach employment certificates.
